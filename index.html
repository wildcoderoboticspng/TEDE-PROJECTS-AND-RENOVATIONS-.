About Us

Tede Projects is a professional home and office renovation company. We merge structural renovation with high-end interior design and rigorous quality control to ensure your spaces are modern, beautiful, and valuable. From minor updates to full property improvements, we handle every job with clean, expert workmanship.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of expert building, design, and renovation services for your property:

Tiling

Perfect wall and floor tiling combining precision design and quality materials for neat lines and strong finishes.

from R150/m² 1 - 5 days
Surface preparation, professional tile leveling, precision cutting, grouting, and thorough post-installation site cleanup.

Painting

Neat interior and exterior painting services executed with high quality paints to elevate your home design.

from R25/m² 2 - 7 days
Crack filling, sanding, primer application, and two premium coats of weather-resistant paint with complete floor protection.

Ceilings

Modern ceiling board installations, structural level repairs, and custom drop-ceilings for advanced room design.

from R120/m² 1 - 4 days
Timber brandering framework, secure ceiling board suspension, tape-and-jointing, and smooth sanding ready for paint.

Rhinoliting

Professional plastering delivering glass-smooth surfaces designed to optimize your renovation finish.

from R80/m² 1 - 3 days
Plaster bonding agent application, precision skim coat application, corner bead alignment, and ultra-smooth finishing.

Paving

Strong paving installations for driveways and courtyards, marrying exterior durability with aesthetic design.

from R200/m² 2 - 6 days
Earth excavation, sub-base compaction, edge-beam installation, paver laying, and joint-filling river sand compaction.

Dry Walling

Quick and neat interior room divisions and office partitioning tailored to flexible spatial design.

from R180/m² 1 - 3 days
Galvanized steel stud framing, acoustic insulation fitting, plasterboard screw-fixing, and joint plastering.

Wooden Floors

Premium laminate and solid wood floor installations engineered for lasting quality and superior room style.

from R250/m² 1 - 4 days
Underlay moisture barrier laying, precision click-lock installation, expansion gap spacing, and skirting board attachment.
